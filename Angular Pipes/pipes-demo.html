Date Pipe example
Today's date :- {{ presentDate | date:'fullDate' }}
This is a short date:- {{ presentDate | date:'shortDate' }}
This line shows the full date:- {{ presentDate | date:'fullDate' }}
This will show a formatted date:- {{ presentDate | date:'dd/MM/yyyy' }}
This indicates the hours and minutes:- {{ presentDate | date:'hh:mm a' }}
